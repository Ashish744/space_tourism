Frequently Asked

Questions before lift-off

What happens if weather changes the launch window?

Our team monitors conditions continuously and will notify you immediately with the next confirmed launch slot.

Do travelers need special training before departure?

Yes, every traveler completes a tailored preparation program, including simulations, physical conditioning, and mission briefings.

Yes, we provide guest access packages for launch-day viewing, hospitality lounges, and secure transport to the site.
